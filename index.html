👶 Imran Khan's Childhood to Youth

Imran Ahmed Khan Niazi was born on October 5, 1952, in Lahore, Pakistan. He studied at Aitchison College, Royal Grammar School Worcester, and later graduated from Oxford in 1975. During his youth, he showed leadership, athletic spirit, and a passion for justice.

🏏 Imran Khan's Cricket Life

Imran Khan began his international cricket career in 1971. His leadership shone brightest when he led Pakistan to its first Cricket World Cup victory in 1992. Known for determination and charisma, he became a cricket legend.

⚖️ Imran Khan's Politics

In 1996, Imran Khan founded Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI). His mission was justice and equality. In 2018, he became the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan, working for education, healthcare, and anti-corruption reforms.

🏥 Shaukat Khanum Hospital

In 1994, he established the Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital after his mother’s death. It provides free cancer treatment to thousands of patients every year, showing compassion and service to humanity.

🌍 Agreements & Love for the People

As Prime Minister, Imran Khan promoted peace and diplomacy with countries like China, Turkey, and Saudi Arabia. Domestically, he focused on welfare programs and national unity, connecting deeply with the people of Pakistan.
